Capybara Lovers

Welcome to the Capybara Lovers website! Here you can find all the information you need about capybaras, the world's largest rodent. Capybaras are native to South America and are known for their friendly and social nature. They are often found near water and are excellent swimmers. Capybaras are herbivores and eat a diet of grasses and aquatic plants. They are also known to be very vocal animals, making a variety of sounds to communicate with each other. If you love capybaras as much as we do, you've come to the right place!
